What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence is the ability of machines to simulate human intelligence, including learning, reasoning, and decision-making.

History

AI began in the 1950s with early research and has rapidly evolved, especially after 2010 due to big data and powerful computing.

Applications

  • Healthcare (diagnosis & robots)
  • Self-driving cars
  • Smart assistants (like Siri)
  • Cybersecurity

Future Impact

AI will transform industries, create new jobs, and reshape how we live and work.
